About Dr.Kurban

Dr. Kurban received his Doctor of Medicine from the University of Aleppo. He completed an internal medicine residency at Louisiana State University Health Services Center. Following his residency, he completed an endocrinology and diabetes fellowship at the University of Florida.

Dr. Kurban served on the diabetes steering committee in Florida. He is a member of the American Thyroid Association and the American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists.

His clinical interests include helping people with thyroid disease and diabetes. He also has contributed many articles in the field of diabetes, weight-loss, and thyroid nodules.
