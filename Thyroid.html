Understanding Thyroid Health

What is the Thyroid?

The thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gland located at the base of your neck. As part of the endocrine system, it produces hormones that regulate your body's metabolism, including heart rate and how quickly you burn calories.

Thyroid disorders can affect your physical and mental health in many ways. Dr. Kurban specializes in diagnosing and treating all types of thyroid conditions.

Common Thyroid Conditions

Hypothyroidism

This occurs when your thyroid doesn't produce enough hormones. Symptoms include fatigue, increased sensitivity to cold, constipation, dry skin, and unexplained weight gain.

Hyperthyroidism

This happens when your thyroid produces too much hormone. Symptoms include weight loss, rapid heartbeat, increased appetite, nervousness, and trouble sleeping.

Thyroid Nodules

These are growths that form within your thyroid. Most are not serious and don't cause symptoms, but they should be evaluated to rule out cancer.

Treatment Options

Treatment depends on the specific condition but may include medication, radioactive iodine therapy, or surgery. During your consultation, Dr. Kurban will perform a comprehensive evaluation and recommend the most appropriate treatment plan for your situation.

Regular monitoring is essential for managing thyroid conditions effectively. Dr. Kurban works closely with his patients to ensure optimal thyroid function and overall health.

Expert Thyroid Care

If you're experiencing symptoms that might be related to thyroid dysfunction, don't wait. Early diagnosis and treatment can prevent complications.
